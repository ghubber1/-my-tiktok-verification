Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the FunUpdates application handles information when you use our service to distribute video content to your TikTok account.

1. Information We Collect

Our application interacts directly with your TikTok account using official APIs. We only request and access permissions that you explicitly grant. We do not store or collect any personal information on remote servers. All authorization tokens (such as access_token) and account keys are saved locally on your own computer/device configuration files.

2. How We Use Your Information

The tokens and authorization codes retrieved from TikTok are used solely for the purpose of uploading and publishing videos from your Telegram channel/group to your authorized TikTok account. No third parties have access to this information.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data, including download video files and configuration credentials, is stored strictly on your local machine. We do not run any central database or cloud service that hosts your credentials or media files.

4. Data Deletion and Revocation

You can revoke access to this application at any time by going to your TikTok account settings (Security -> Manage App Permissions) and removing access for this application. To wipe local data, you can delete the telegram_video_distributor_config.json file on your computer.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our official website.
